1.6 Create Annotations

1.6.1 Create Class Annotation

Regular Annotate:

1)1) Highlight a span of text corresponding to a "Tums"mention.

2)2) Select "New Treatment" from the pop-up dialogue to create the new annotation.

Fast Annotate

1)Right-click the colored box next to "Problem".

 

2)Highlight a span of text corresponding to a "Problem" mention to create the new annotation.

3)Repeat step 2 for multiple "Problem" annotations.

4)Right-click the colored box next to "Problem" to unselect, or another class to switch to another class.
