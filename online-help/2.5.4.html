2.5.4.2 Delete Relationships

1) Click to open Relationship Schema Editor.

2) Select the relationship.

3) Click “Remove Selected” to delete relationship.to open Attribute Editor

 

2.5.4.3 Modify Relationship

1) Click to open Relationship Schema Editor.

2) Select the relationship.

3) Click “Edit” to edit.

4) Type in new Regular Expression and new Relationship Name. 5) Click “Save” to save the relationship.
